Deck I

WHY

ARK is not a place. It is an idea.

A container for people separated by the storms of life — offering a space away from the flood, away from the noise.

On the dancefloor, strangers become partners: same BPM, same sync, moving toward one another — and toward a safe shore where hope outruns the gaps.

Deck II

WHAT

Independent electronic label

ARK curates and releases electronic music from artists of all identities and backgrounds — from the Middle East and beyond.

Collective + non-profit movement

Every track, every collaboration, every event is proof that music can move, connect, and carry change back into real life.

Deck III

HOW

50 / 50 — transparent

All profits from streams, downloads, and purchases are allocated clearly: 50% to artists · 50% to humanitarian, apolitical causes supporting liberal values, including LGBTQ+ and women’s rights in the Middle East.

A real plan

Target: 50 albums in 5 years (10 per year). Each release is a curated VA/album — a soundtrack that’s bigger than music.
